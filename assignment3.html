What is Retail Automation?

Retail Automation is about using technology to improve customer satisfaction and experience during shopping in a supermarket.
It eliminates lines, wait times, and reduces human errors, making it more efficient for both businesses and customers.

How is AI Being Implemented Into Retail?

Walmart, one of the larget retailers in the U.S. uses AI, to detect when certain shelves need to be restocked, and sends robots to go and fill the empty shelves. This is much more effiecient, than sending a worker to go and do this themself, and find the stock needed.
