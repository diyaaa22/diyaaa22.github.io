There are many ethical issues with Artificial Intelligence retail automation in supermarkets. These include job displacement, privacy or data security breaches, bias from training data, lack of transparency, customer manipulation and exploitation, over relying on technology, unintended results. All of these issues are pretty important for supermarket chains to think about and address before adopting Artificial Intelligence in their supermarkets as they will be held responsible if anything goes wrong. Job displacement is currently a huge ethical issue that most people are worried about. However, it is important to note that there are many things that cannot be automated by Artifical Intelligence and that people are needed to complete a lot of tasks that Artificial Intelligence can’t do. Privacy and data security breaches are also a huge concern as Artificial Intelligence collects a lot of customer data and most customers would be worried about any potential chances of misuse of their personal data. Artificial Intelligence systems are also trained by people making it an issue and unethical if the training data discriminates against certain people or products in a supermarket and provides incorrect data just due to the training data stating unethical things. It would also affect customer service if the Artificial Intelligence system is taught to discriminate against people. Lack of transparency comes into play here as Artificial Intelligence algorithms and the training data may make it harder for people to understand how it is providing information causing people to not believe the Artificial Intelligence model/ virtual assistant. Another unethical point is customer manipulation which basically contradicts the lack of transparency point but it also depends on the algorithm and what it has learnt through the training data. Overreliance on technology is a huge issue as it may cause people to soon lose touch with reality and cause poor judgement and decisions for certain things. Overall, it will be interesting to see how everything plays out with the future of AI expanding rapidly.
